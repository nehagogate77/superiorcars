Our automobile business started in 1987, in Brampton. At the start of the business, our rental company had only small compact and economy cars. As the business grew over the years, our company started getting larger vehicles such as compact SUVs, standard SUVs, full-size SUVS, minivans, pickup trucks and luxury vehicles.
